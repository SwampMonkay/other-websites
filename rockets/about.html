About Us

By Rocket Staff Member



What do we do?


Rockets is an organization that focuses on studying nature and finding out as much as we can about nature in the Swamps. We have sheets of paper, and you collect small samples of things you find in the Swamps. Once we have samples, we tape them on the paper and write as much as we can about them (like questions, facts, and hypotheses). We then put our paper in a binder under the correct section.


Meetings:


During our meetings, we get out the binder and each take turns sharing the papers we put in there. As we share, members give each other advice and compliments. After that, we put the binder away and talk about future plans. Meetings are held under Juliet’s sunroom, and we will tell you when a meeting is coming up.

What can I do?


If you join our club, you can collect samples as one of your first jobs.You can also contribute to the decorations in our area if you want. Once we think you are ready (which will be pretty quickly) you will start to make papers of your own and you will get a section in the binder. You will be trusted to do your own thing, but don’t be in the area while Juliet is not around.
